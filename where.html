Where on Earth?

Packaging Design + Branding / Ginger Fox

“Where on Earth? is a globe-trotting game of travel trivia! Answer your way around the world in this travel-tastic trivia game. To win bonus points, test your geographical knowledge even further by locating your answers on the map of the world.”

A geographical trivia game by Ginger Fox, designed by me. Spherical packaging styled to accurately represent a globe. Takes influence from explorer-era typography, mixed with bright, modern colours.
