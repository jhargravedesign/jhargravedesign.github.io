Where on Earth?

“Where on Earth? is a globe-trotting game of travel trivia! Answer your way around the world in this travel-tastic trivia game. To win bonus points, test your geographical knowledge even further by locating your answers on the map of the world. The perfect gift for travel lovers, geography fans and trivia buffs this makes a great family game for players aged 12 years and over. Where on Earth? is packaged in a quirky globe-shaped box.”

This design presented a technical challenge to get an accurate globe look to the packaging. While it seems a similar shape to a real globe, the construction and shape makes it a different deal entirely. By using map projection software (QGIS) I was able to create two different maps using a polar projection, centred around the North and South pole, these were then stitched together with the middle band of the box created from a regular rectangular map. I’m proud of the result, as it represents a challenge I was able to overcome through learning something new. I love the box for this game as it conveys what the games about so directly.
