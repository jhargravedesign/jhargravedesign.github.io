Which One?

Packaging + Branding / Ginger Fox

Which One? is a trivia game with some big differences. Match up to five answers with five questions correctly to win points; which one goes with which? 

Sounds simple, but there’s a twist! If any of your answers are incorrect, you lose all your points for the round! Will you gamble on an answer or play it safe? 

A trivia game by Ginger Fox, packaging and branding designed by me. Inspired by Saul Bass to create a dynamic and bold presence. Twisting arrows represent the indecision you'll feel while debating whether to risk a solid three points for a risky five.
