Celestial Tales

Book Design + Illustration / Personal

There are 88 constellations, each with their own tale, a reason to why a few dots in the sky are joined to represent a creature, king or monster.

Celestial Tales is a book about those figures and their stories.

A personal project, mythology, astronomy and their connection fascinates me. The drive for humans to make sense of the unknown has led to the stars being a source of intrigue for millenia. Long before we could start to understand them, we have looked up at the stars and joined their dots to create patterns and into these patterns we have embeded countless stories.

Celestial Tales is an ongoing project in which I hope to encompass all 88 constellations, for now it contains those of the Perseus and Orion families. Each constellation features an illustraion set on a star map and the story attributed to that figure.
