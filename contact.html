About

As a junior graphic designer with nearly three years commercial experience within a Cheltenham-based games and gifts company, my primary expertise is woking with print. I also have experience with screen based work and built this website from scratch in HTML5, CSS3 and Jquery.

I have strong technical skills with Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, inDesign, XD and am confident in after effects. Experience in working for both print and screen has given me knowledge of printing processes, practices and standards. I'm always passionate about expanding my ability and skills and would love to hear from anyone in need of creative work.
