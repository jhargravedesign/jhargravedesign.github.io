Ore

Packaging + Branding / 1HQ

1HQ is a brand-agency based in Windsor, England. Their headquarters are in a former brewery building so their building features an in-house bar - and if you have an in-house bar, you should have an in-house, branded beer.

With the offer of an internship as a reward, 1HQ challenged the students of the Graphic Design course of the Univesity of Gloucestershire to pitch our designs.

In partnership with my friend and fellow designer, Josh Eyles, we created a brand focused around the old-english name for Windsor, 'Windles-Ore', meaning 'winch by the river'. This is represented by various iconography in the design. Ore, is like an idea in the creative process, it takes time and craft to form it into a finished product.

We set our garden on fire to promote our new beer, and got those internships at 1HQ.
