Twisted Puzzle Books

These two puzzle books offer over a hundred puzzles each, but none are standard, each involve a different take on the puzzle with new rules that ‘twist’ the way you have to solve them.

This range of puzzle books are something new in their field, they subvert expectations and challenge you to think differently about a problem you might have encountered hundreds of times before. I wanted the design to be something different too, something that really stands alone on the shelf surrounded by other puzzle books.

The internals needed to work for a range of copy length and puzzle variations without requiring hours of manual tweaking for each page. As Ginger Fox often has their books translated for foreign markets, the text needed to be set in one-colour black to reduce the plate changes, and with enough space to allow for a different language causing the text to be longer.
