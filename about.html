About Me

I'm Jamie Hargrave, a multi-disciplinary designer based in Bristol, UK.

I graduated from the University of Gloucestershire in 2015 with a BA (Hons) in Graphic Design and have since worked as a junior designer in the Ginger Fox (a games and gifts company) design team for nearly three years.

Experienced in both print and screen design, I offer a wide range of services, including packaging design, branding, website design, and much more. If there's something you need designing, I'd love to hear from you at jamiehargravedesigns@gmail.com.
