Placing the Past

Packaging + Branding / Ginger Fox

The game of time and risk. Think you know the correct chronological order of the historical events, then play your hand - but get any wrong and score nothing. Other players placed theirs incorrectly? Call them out for bonus points with the challenge bell.

History based trivia game by Ginger Fox, designed by me. Bold, neon packaging to make chronology more exciting than it sounds. A timeline wraps around the box, depicting a selection of events using icons - gives you a mini-game to guess them, gives me a cheat-sheet so I can score some cheap points.
