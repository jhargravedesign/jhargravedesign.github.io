Placing the Past

“The game of time and risk. Think you know the correct chronological order of the historical events, then play your hand - but get any wrong and score nothing. Other players placed theirs incorrectly? Call them out for bonus points with the challenge bell.”

The design features a winding timeline that wraps around the box, linking up with the title on the lid. Along the timeline sit icons representing various historical events found within the game’s contents. I wanted to create some interactivity with the box itself - can you work out what the icons represent? The wrapping of the timeline of the box also encourages the holder to turn it around in their hands. Before the box is in someone’s hands however, it needs to stand out on the shelf, the bold, neon colours set against black hope to achieve this.

While playing the game, the cards are laid out, black side up, before they are flipped to reveal their chronology. The bold aqua colour creates a ‘reveal’ with the contrast against the black and yellow on the front of the card.
