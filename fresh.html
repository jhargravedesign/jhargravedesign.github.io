Fresh {UoG}

Event Design + Branding / UoG

UCAS host the annual 'Design Your Future' (now 'Create Your Future') event for universities across the UK to promote their art and design courses to prospective students.

The University of Gloucestershire needed a stall designed, it needed to capture the attention of the students, advertise the Universities' courses and collect the prospective students' contact information.

As students at UoG, myself, Josh Eyles and Chris Seaborn designed a lemonade themed stall. With a lemonade stand that functioned as a handout station, where prospective students could exchange contact details for goodie bags of lemon sweets, course information and lemonade.

Bright yellow walls and crisp typography meant the stand stood out from across the event space, giving the University of Gloucestershire its most successful year at Design your Future yet.
